Project Overview

Created a MATLAB GUI for measuring diameters of round objects in images, utilizing the Image Processing Toolbox.

Achieved 95% accuracy in diameter measurements compared to manual methods. Reduced analysis time by 75% compared to traditional measurement techniques.
